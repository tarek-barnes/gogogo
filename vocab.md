### 囲碁用語

## Fuseki
- 締まり, シマリ = corner enclosure
- カカリ, 掛かり = corner approach
- ケイマガカリ = one space low approach
- いっけんたがかり = one space high approach
- おげいまがかり = two space low approach
- にけんたがかり = two space high approach
- 裾がかり（すそがかり）= skirt approach  (classic 2-5 invasion on 4-4)
- 両ガカリ（りょうがかり）= double approach
- ハサミ = pincer

## Haeng-ma
- 一間トビ（いっけんとび）= one space jump
    > トビ = jump
    > 間 = literally space, measures distance between stones
- 二間トビ（にけんとび）= two space jump
- ハザマトビ = diagonal jump / elephants move (with elephant eye)
    > 狭（はざま）= interval, space
- ヒラキ = extension
- ヒラキヅメ = checking extension (one that serves dual purpose, like also threatens an invasion)
- 伸び, ノビ = stretch / extend (usually towards center)
- サガリ, 下がり = similar to 伸び, but away from center, towards side
    > root verb: 下がる（さがる）= to fall, drop, sink, hang, withdraw
- マガリ, 曲がり = turn
- 割込み, ワリコミ = wedge
- ノゾキ = peep
    > やぐらノゾキ = double peep
- ツギ = connect (locally)
    > use 連絡（れんらく）as a strategic term, as in group A and B are connected
- 掛け継ぎ, カケツギ = hanging connection (usually via tiger mouth)
- 堅継ぎ, カタツギ = solid connection
- 二丁ツギ（にちょうつぎ）= bamboo connection

## Shapes
- 猫の顔（ねこのかお）= tiger mouth
- トックリ形（とっくりがた）= sake bottle shape (一間トビ with けいま in the middle)
    > 犬の顔（いぬのかお）= dog face, synonym for トックリ形
- 馬の顔（うまのかお）= horse head (一間トビ with おげいま in the middle)
- 急所（きゅうしょ）= vital point
